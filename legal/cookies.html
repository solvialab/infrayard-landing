Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Summary

We currently do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or behavioral tracking on the public Infrayard website.

Strictly necessary and preference storage

The site may use browser storage, such as localStorage, to remember your light/dark theme preference. This is used only to make the website work as expected and does not track you across websites.

Forms and external services

When you submit a form, the data is sent to FormSubmit so the message can be delivered to us. External resources such as CDN-hosted CSS/icons and external social links may involve requests to third-party domains, but we do not use them for advertising or analytics tracking.

No cookie banner right now

Because we do not currently set non-essential analytics or marketing cookies, we do not show a cookie consent banner. If we add analytics, retargeting, embedded media, or other non-essential tracking later, we should add consent controls before those tools load.

How to control storage

You can clear cookies and site data in your browser settings at any time. Clearing site data may reset your theme preference.

Contact

Questions about cookies or privacy can be sent to hello@infrayard.eu.
